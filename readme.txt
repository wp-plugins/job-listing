Easily add The Ideal Candidate job widget as a Wordpress sidebar widget.

== Description ==

This simple widget plugin for your sidebar displays The Ideal Candidate affiliate widget box with your affiliate code associated with it.

The widget box can be customized on The Ideal Candidate site to how you want it to look with your branding, it will the display a list of jobs from the network for your visitors to view.

Any employers who click on your widget box and posts a job will earn you a percentage of the sale.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `theidealcandidate` directory to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. Navigate to your sidebar widget area, in 2.7.1 this is under the Appearance tab.
1. Add `The Ideal Candidate` to the sidebar of your choice.
1. Configure the widget, this may require you to register with The Ideal Candidate (it's free).
